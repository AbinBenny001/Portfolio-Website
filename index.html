All About me:

Bruce Wayne is a wealthy American industrialist.He was born to wealthy physician Dr. Thomas Wayne and his wife Martha, who were themselves members of the prestigious Wayne and Kane families of Gotham City, respectively.As a child, Bruce witnessed the murder of his parents.He grew up home-schooled on his parents' Wayne Manor estate.he was taken care of by Alfred. Alfred Pennyworth is Bruce Wayne's loyal and tireless butler, housekeeper, legal guardian, best friend and father figure following the murders of his parents. Fueled by the loss of his parents, Bruce began a global sojourn, his first stop being in Tibet where he trained in martial arts and sword fighting. the age of 19, he learned how to race cars in life-or-death situations . At age 21, Bruce studied the ins and outs of technological gadgetry Taking this lesson to heart, and after seven long years of absence, Bruce finally returned to Gotham[23] at the age of 25.
